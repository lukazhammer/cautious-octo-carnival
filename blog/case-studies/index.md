## Common Success Patterns

**1. Narrowing Focus**
Every successful transformation involved narrowing target from "everyone" to specific segment.

**2. Premium Pricing**
All case studies showed pricing increases of 30-100%+ after repositioning.

**3. Strategic Patience**
Results visible in 3-6 months, significant impact in 12-18 months. Consistency required.

**4. Data-Driven Decisions**
Best transformations analyzed customer data to find positioning opportunities.
