## Customer Health Scoring

### Health Score Calculation

**Engagement Metrics (40%)**:
- Days active (last 30 days)
- Features used
- Modules completed
- Time in app

**Progress Metrics (30%)**:
- Onboarding completion
- Module completion rate
- Content created
- Goals achieved

**Satisfaction Metrics (20%)**:
- NPS score
- Support ticket sentiment
- Feature request activity
- Community participation

**Business Metrics (10%)**:
- Plan tier
- Payment status
- Contract length
- Upsell potential

### Health Score Actions

**Green (80-100)**: Happy customer
- Continue nurture
- Request testimonial
- Identify upsell opportunities
- Invite to advocacy program

**Yellow (50-79)**: At risk
- Monitor closely
- Proactive outreach
- Offer assistance
- Identify friction points

**Red (0-49)**: Critical
- Immediate CSM intervention
- Executive escalation (if strategic)
- Win-back campaign
- Exit interview if churning

## Churn Prevention

### Early Warning Signs
- 7+ days inactive
- Decreased login frequency
- Support ticket spike
- NPS score drop
- Feature usage decline
- Payment failed

### Intervention Strategies

**Education**:
- Send tutorial videos
- Offer training webinar
- Provide use case guides
- Share success stories

**Support**:
- Offer 1-on-1 call
- Assign dedicated CSM
- Priority support access
- Custom onboarding

**Value**:
- Showcase ROI
- Share new features
- Provide industry insights
- Offer advanced resources

**Incentive**:
- Special pricing
- Feature upgrade
- Extended trial
- Service credits
