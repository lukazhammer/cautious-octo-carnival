## Messaging Dos & Don'ts

### Do:
✓ Focus on outcomes (clarity, growth, confidence)
✓ Be specific about what users get
✓ Use concrete examples
✓ Lead with value, not features
✓ Address pain points directly
✓ Back claims with evidence
✓ Speak to different audience segments appropriately

### Don't:
✗ Overpromise results
✗ Use vague claims ("best," "revolutionary")
✗ Rely on jargon or buzzwords
✗ Dismiss the complexity of brand strategy
✗ Undervalue professional consulting
✗ Claim instant or effortless results
✗ Use fear-based messaging

## Messaging Testing & Refinement

### A/B Test Variations

**Test different angles:**
- Outcome-focused vs. process-focused
- Professional vs. approachable tone
- Feature-led vs. benefit-led
- Problem-first vs. solution-first

**Track metrics:**
- Click-through rates
- Conversion rates
- Time on page
- Bounce rates
- Form completions

**Iterate based on:**
- Quantitative data (metrics above)
- Qualitative feedback (user surveys)
- Support questions (indicates confusion)
- Sales objections (reveals concerns)
