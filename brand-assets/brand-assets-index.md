## Motion Design

### Motion Guidelines

| Document | Location | Contents |
|----------|----------|----------|
| **Motion Design** | `/design-templates/motion-design-guidelines.md` | Animation principles, timing, transitions, interactions |

**Covered:**
- **Timing & Duration:** Micro to page transitions (100ms - 1000ms)
- **Easing Functions:** Ease-in, ease-out, ease-in-out, linear
- **Transition Types:** Fade, slide, scale, expand/collapse, blur
- **Micro-Interactions:** Button press, hover, toggle, focus, checkbox
- **Loading States:** Spinner, progress bar, skeleton screens, pulsing dots
- **Page Transitions:** Route changes, scroll animations
- **Modal & Overlays:** Appearance, dismissal, dropdowns, toasts
- **Success & Error:** Checkmark animations, shake effects
- **Accessibility:** Reduced motion support

**Performance:**
- 60 FPS target
- GPU-accelerated properties (transform, opacity)
- Respects `prefers-reduced-motion`

## Usage Guidelines

### For Internal Team

**Accessing assets:**
1. Consult Quick Reference Guide for daily needs
2. Reference full Style Guide for detailed specs
3. Use templates as starting points
4. Contact brand team for custom requests

**Creating new materials:**
1. Review relevant sections of style guide
2. Use existing templates when possible
3. Follow color, typography, and voice guidelines
4. Maintain brand consistency
5. Test accessibility (color contrast, readability)

---

### For External Partners

**Asset packages available:**
1. **Logo Package:** All logo variations, multiple formats
2. **Brand Guidelines PDF:** Condensed style guide (request from brand team)
3. **Template Kit:** Relevant templates for your project
4. **Icon Library:** SVG icon package

**Requesting access:**
- Email: brand@brandora.com
- Specify: What you're creating, which assets needed
- Allow: 2-3 business days for package preparation
