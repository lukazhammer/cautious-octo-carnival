## Typography

*For complete details, see: `/style-guide/sections/03-typography.md`*

### Font Families

**Headings:** Montserrat (Semi Bold 600, Bold 700)
- Professional, modern, geometric sans-serif
- All headings (H1-H6), navigation, buttons

**Body:** Poppins (Regular 400, Medium 500, Semi Bold 600)
- Readable, friendly, approachable
- Body copy, UI text, captions

**Monospace:** JetBrains Mono
- Code snippets, technical content

### Type Scale (Desktop)

- **H1:** Montserrat Semi Bold, 56px / 3.5rem
- **H2:** Montserrat Semi Bold, 40px / 2.5rem
- **H3:** Montserrat Semi Bold, 32px / 2rem
- **H4:** Montserrat Semi Bold, 24px / 1.5rem
- **H5:** Montserrat Semi Bold, 20px / 1.25rem
- **H6:** Montserrat Semi Bold, 16px / 1rem
- **Body Large:** Poppins Regular, 20px / 1.25rem
- **Body Regular:** Poppins Regular, 16px / 1rem
- **Body Small:** Poppins Regular, 14px / 0.875rem
- **Caption:** Poppins Regular, 12px / 0.75rem

### Guidelines
- Line height: 1.5-1.625 for body text, 1.1-1.4 for headings
- Line length: 60-75 characters optimal
- Letter spacing: Negative for large headings, positive for small text
- Color contrast: Minimum WCAG AA (4.5:1), prefer AAA (7:1)

## Brand Voice & Writing

*For complete details, see: `/style-guide/sections/04-brand-voice.md`*

### Voice Characteristics
- **Clear** (not clever)
- **Empowering** (not prescriptive)
- **Conversational but professional**
- **Confident without arrogance**
- **Data-informed and human**

### Writing Style
- Active voice over passive
- Concise sentences (15-20 words target)
- Avoid jargon unless audience-appropriate
- Use contractions for conversational tone
- Inclusive language (they/them, folks, everyone)

### Tone by Context
- **Marketing:** Inspiring + Clear
- **Product UI:** Helpful + Concise
- **Education:** Insightful + Accessible
- **Support:** Empathetic + Solution-Focused
- **Social Media:** Engaging + Authentic

### Grammar & Mechanics
- Oxford comma (always)
- Sentence case for headings
- One space after periods
- Numbers: Spell out 1-9, numerals for 10+
- Contractions: Use in most contexts (except legal)

## Photography & Imagery

*For complete details, see: `/photography/photography-direction.md`*

### Photography Style
- Professional yet approachable
- Authentic, not overly staged
- Diverse and inclusive
- Natural lighting preferred
- Bright and airy

### Subject Matter
- Entrepreneurs and professionals (25-50 years)
- Modern workspaces (offices, coffee shops)
- Strategy sessions and collaboration
- Technology and screens (platform in use)

### Color Treatment
- Slightly cool tone (aligns with brand blues)
- Natural skin tones maintained
- Bright, airy exposure
- Avoid heavy filters

### Post-Processing
- Exposure: +0.3 to +0.7 (bright)
- Contrast: +10 to +20
- Highlights: -10 to -20
- Shadows: +20 to +40
- Clarity: +10 to +20

## Motion Design

*For complete details, see: `/design-templates/motion-design-guidelines.md`*

### Timing
- **Micro-interactions:** 100-200ms
- **UI transitions:** 200-300ms
- **Page transitions:** 300-500ms
- **Maximum duration:** 1000ms

### Easing
- **Ease-out:** Elements entering
- **Ease-in:** Elements exiting
- **Ease-in-out:** General transitions
- **Linear:** Loading indicators

### Transition Types
- Fade: Appearing/disappearing content
- Slide: Panels, menus, drawers
- Scale: Modals, popovers
- Expand/Collapse: Accordions, details

### Accessibility
- Respect `prefers-reduced-motion`
- No blocking animations
- Focus indicators always visible

## Digital Applications

### Website Standards (Bauhaus Design System)

**Brandora website implements Bauhaus design principles:**

**Core Principles:**
- **Geometric design language** - Circles, squares, triangles as primary elements
- **Form follows function** - Every element serves a purpose
- **Grid-based rationality** - 12-column systematic layouts
- **Bold, high contrast** - Navy, Blue, Yellow on White
- **Asymmetric balance** - Dynamic yet organized compositions
- **Minimalist aesthetic** - Remove the unnecessary

**Technical Standards:**
- Responsive design (mobile-first)
- WCAG AA accessibility minimum
- Brand colors applied through Bauhaus lens
- Geometric component library
- Performance optimized (60 FPS animations)
- Pure CSS geometric shapes
- 8px spacing system

**Key Bauhaus Elements:**
- Geometric shapes as decorative accents
- Color blocking (Navy, Yellow, Blue sections)
- Asymmetric layouts (60/40, 2/3 + 1/3 splits)
- Bauhaus typography (large, bold, geometric)
- Minimal borders and shadows
- Rational grid systems

**See:** `/design-templates/website-components/bauhaus-design-system.md` for complete specifications

### App Interface
- Consistent with website components
- Platform-specific adaptations (iOS, Android)
- Brand colors and iconography
- Accessible and inclusive
