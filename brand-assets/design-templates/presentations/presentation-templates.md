# Presentation Templates

## Overview

**Three complete presentation decks:**
1. Pitch Deck (15 slides)
2. Product Demo (20 slides)
3. Educational Webinar (25 slides)

**Design specifications:**
- Aspect ratio: 16:9 (1920×1080 pixels or 10×5.625 inches)
- Fonts: Montserrat (headings), Poppins (body)
- Colors: Brand palette
- Format: PowerPoint (.pptx), Google Slides, Keynote

## Master Slide Layouts

### Title Slide Layout
**Elements:**
- Full-width background (White or Background Blue)
- Logo top left (150px wide)
- Title center-aligned
- Subtitle below title
- Footer with date/presenter

### Section Header Slide
**Elements:**
- Large section number/name
- Minimal design
- Navy or Blue accent
- Prepares audience for new section

### Content Slide (Standard)
**Elements:**
- Header area: Title + slide number
- Main content area: Text, images, charts
- Footer: Logo + page number
- Consistent margins (80px all sides)

### Two-Column Layout
**Elements:**
- Header
- Left column: Text or image
- Right column: Complementary content
- Equal or 60/40 split

### Full-Screen Image
**Elements:**
- High-quality image
- Text overlay with readable background
- Minimal design, maximum impact

### Quote Slide
**Elements:**
- Large quote text center-aligned
- Attribution
- Quotation marks (visual)
- Accent color background or minimal design

## Design Guidelines

### Typography Hierarchy
- **Slide titles:** Montserrat Semi Bold, 44pt, Navy
- **Section headers:** Montserrat Bold, 56pt, Navy
- **Body text:** Poppins Regular, 20-24pt, Neutral 900
- **Captions:** Poppins Regular, 16pt, Neutral 700
- **Numbers/stats:** Montserrat Extra Bold, 72-120pt, Blue

### Color Usage
- **Backgrounds:** White, Background Blue #F1F9FF
- **Accent backgrounds:** Navy #070058 (use sparingly)
- **Text:** Navy #070058, Neutral 900 #0F172A
- **Highlights:** Blue #6F8AEC, Yellow #FFBE44
- **Charts/graphs:** Brand palette

### Imagery
- High-quality, professional photos
- Consistent style across deck
- Proper licensing
- Alt text for accessibility

### Data Visualization
- Use brand colors
- Clear labels
- Readable from distance
- Simple, not cluttered
- Source citations

### Animations (Use Sparingly)
- **Builds:** Bullet points appear one at a time
- **Transitions:** Subtle fade or none
- **Emphasis:** Zoom or highlight
- **Avoid:** Distracting animations, excessive movement
