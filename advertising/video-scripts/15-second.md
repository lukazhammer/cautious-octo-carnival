## Production Notes for 15-Second Videos

### Pacing:
- **Words Per Minute:** Max 180 (3 words per second)
- **Scene Changes:** 2-3 scenes maximum
- **Transitions:** Quick cuts or fast fades (200ms)
- **No dead time:** Every second counts

### Visual Strategy:
- **Hook Image:** First 1 second must grab attention
- **Clear Visuals:** Simple, not complex
- **Text Overlays:** Large, readable, minimal
- **Brand Elements:** Logo in first or last 3 seconds
- **CTA:** Visible last 5 seconds minimum

### Audio:
- **Voiceover:** Clear, energetic, fast-paced
- **Music:** Optional, if used keep it subtle
- **Sound Effects:** Minimal, purposeful only
- **Mixing:** Voice at 100%, music at 20-30%

### Mobile Optimization:
- **Vertical Format:** 9:16 for Stories/Reels
- **Square Format:** 1:1 for Feed
- **Large Text:** Readable on small screens
- **Captions:** Always include (sound often off)

### Platform Specs:

**Instagram/Facebook Stories:**
- Aspect Ratio: 9:16 (1080 x 1920)
- Format: MP4
- Max File Size: 4GB
- Length: 15 seconds exactly

**Instagram/Facebook Feed:**
- Aspect Ratio: 1:1 (1080 x 1080)
- Format: MP4
- Max File Size: 4GB
- Length: Up to 60 seconds (15 recommended)

**Twitter:**
- Aspect Ratio: 1:1 or 16:9
- Format: MP4 or MOV
- Max File Size: 512MB
- Length: Up to 2:20 (15 seconds recommended)

**TikTok:**
- Aspect Ratio: 9:16
- Format: MP4 or MOV
- Max File Size: 287.6MB
- Length: Up to 60 seconds (15 seconds performs well)

**YouTube Pre-Roll (Skippable):**
- Aspect Ratio: 16:9
- Format: MP4
- Resolution: 1920 x 1080
- Length: 15-20 seconds (skippable after 5)

**YouTube Bumper (Non-Skippable):**
- Aspect Ratio: 16:9
- Format: MP4
- Resolution: 1920 x 1080
- Length: Exactly 6 seconds (not 15, but good to know)

### Testing Framework:

**Test Variables:**
1. **Hook (0-3 sec):** Question vs. statement vs. visual
2. **Voiceover:** Male vs. female vs. founder voice
3. **Music:** With vs. without
4. **CTA Text:** Different phrasings
5. **Visual Style:** Animated vs. live action vs. screen recording

**Success Metrics:**
- 3-second view rate (Did hook work?)
- Completion rate (Watched all 15 seconds?)
- Click-through rate (CTR)
- Cost per view
- Cost per click (CPC)
- Conversion rate

### Best Practices:

**Do:**
- Start with strongest hook
- Show product/brand early
- Include captions (80% watch silent)
- Test multiple versions
- Keep CTA simple and clear
- End with brand reinforcement

**Don't:**
- Waste the first 3 seconds
- Use complex narratives
- Include too many messages
- Expect people to turn sound on
- Hide the CTA
- Make viewers work to understand

### Creative Refresh:

**Frequency:** Every 2-3 weeks
**Why:** Ad fatigue sets in quickly with short-form
**How:** New hook, different visuals, fresh testimonial
**Test:** Always run 2-3 variations simultaneously
