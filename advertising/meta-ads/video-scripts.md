## Production Notes

### Visual Style Guidelines:
- **Color Palette:** Brandora brand colors (Navy #070058, Blue #6F8AEC, Yellow #FFBE44)
- **Typography:** Montserrat for headlines, Poppins for body
- **Motion:** Smooth, professional transitions (300-500ms)
- **Pace:** Fast enough to maintain attention, slow enough to comprehend
- **Captions:** Always include (80% watch with sound off)

### Founder Casting (if using talent):
- Age: 28-45
- Diverse representation across videos
- Authentic, relatable, not overly polished
- Comfortable on camera but not "salesy"

### Technical Specs:
- **Aspect Ratios:**
  - Feed: 1:1 (square) or 4:5 (vertical)
  - Stories: 9:16 (vertical)
  - Reels: 9:16 (vertical)
- **Resolution:** 1080x1080 minimum (square), 1080x1350 (4:5), 1080x1920 (9:16)
- **File Size:** Under 4GB
- **Length:**
  - Feed: 1-60 seconds (optimal: 15-30)
  - Stories: 1-15 seconds
  - Reels: 15-60 seconds (optimal: 30-45)
- **Frame Rate:** 30fps minimum
- **File Format:** MP4 or MOV

### Music Suggestions:
- Upbeat but professional
- Not distracting from voiceover
- Licensed for commercial use
- Volume: Background level (20-30% of VO)

**Recommended Mood:**
- Inspiring but grounded
- Professional but approachable
- Modern, tech-forward
- Optimistic, possibility-focused

### Testing Framework:
- Create 3-5 second hooks variations
- Test with/without music
- Test different CTAs
- Monitor completion rates by length
- Optimize based on 3-second view rates

### Platform-Specific Variations:

**Instagram Feed:**
- Square 1:1 ratio preferred
- First 3 seconds crucial (autoplay)
- Strong visual hook
- Captions essential

**Instagram Stories:**
- 9:16 vertical format
- Interactive elements (polls, questions)
- Swipe-up CTA (if available)
- 15-second max recommended

**Facebook Feed:**
- 1:1 or 4:5 works well
- Slightly longer acceptable (45-60s)
- Native upload performs better
- Focus on educational value

**Instagram Reels:**
- 9:16 vertical format
- Trending audio consideration
- Fast-paced editing
- Hook in first second
- 30-45 seconds optimal

### Call-to-Action Variations:

**Awareness Stage:**
- "Learn More"
- "See How It Works"
- "Watch Demo"

**Consideration Stage:**
- "Start Free Assessment"
- "Try Free"
- "Get Started"

**Conversion Stage:**
- "Sign Up Now"
- "Start Building"
- "Get Your Strategy"

## A/B Testing Plan

### Test Variables:
1. **Hook Variations:**
   - Question vs. statement
   - Problem vs. solution
   - Stat vs. story

2. **Length:**
   - 15s vs. 30s vs. 60s
   - Impact on completion rate
   - Impact on conversion

3. **Talent:**
   - Real founders vs. actors
   - Different demographics
   - Speaking style variations

4. **Format:**
   - Talking head vs. animation
   - Screen recording vs. live action
   - Mixed media vs. single format

5. **CTA Placement:**
   - End only
   - Beginning + end
   - Throughout

### Success Metrics:
- 3-second view rate (hook effectiveness)
- Average watch time
- Completion rate
- Click-through rate (CTR)
- Cost per click (CPC)
- Conversion rate
- Cost per acquisition (CPA)
- Return on ad spend (ROAS)

### Optimization Timeline:
- Week 1: Test 5 video variations
- Week 2: Identify top 2 performers
- Week 3: Create variations of winners
- Week 4: Scale best performers
- Monthly: Refresh creative to avoid ad fatigue
