## SUBJECT LINE BEST PRACTICES

### Character Length:
- **Optimal:** 40-50 characters
- **Maximum:** 70 characters (mobile cutoff)
- **Minimum:** 25 characters (too short looks spammy)

### Personalization:
- **First Name:** Increases open rates 10-14%
- **Company Name:** Use when relevant
- **Location:** For local/regional campaigns
- **Behavior:** Reference their actions

**Examples:**
- "[Name], your brand assessment is ready"
- "[Company] brand strategy: Next steps"
- "Hey [Name], quick question about your positioning"

### Emojis:
**Use Sparingly:** 1 emoji per subject line maximum

### Power Words:
**Urgency:** Now, Today, Expires, Last Chance, Limited, Ending
**Exclusivity:** Exclusive, Private, Members, Insider, VIP
**Curiosity:** Secret, Revealed, Hidden, Discover, Unlock
**Value:** Free, Save, Bonus, Extra, Plus
**Social Proof:** Proven, Trusted, Popular, Best-selling

### Avoid Spam Triggers:
**Don't Use:**
- ALL CAPS
- Multiple exclamation marks!!!
- Excessive $$$$ symbols
- "Free" too frequently
- RE: or FWD: (when fake)
- Excessive punctuation???
- Misleading claims
- Words like: Guarantee, Winner, Cash, Prize (use carefully)

### Testing Framework:

**A/B Test Variables:**
1. **Length:** Short vs. long
2. **Format:** Question vs. statement
3. **Personalization:** With name vs. without
4. **Emojis:** With vs. without
5. **Tone:** Formal vs. casual
6. **Focus:** Benefit vs. feature vs. urgency

**Test Protocol:**
- Send to minimum 1,000 per variation
- Track open rate (primary metric)
- Track click rate (secondary metric)
- Track unsubscribe rate (watch closely)
- Run test minimum 24-48 hours
- Declare winner at 95% confidence

### Email Types & Expected Open Rates:

**Welcome Emails:** 50-60% (highest open rates)
**Educational Content:** 20-30%
**Product Updates:** 25-35%
