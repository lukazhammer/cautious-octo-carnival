## Testing Framework for Headlines

### A/B Test Methodology:
1. **Traffic Split:** 50/50 minimum
2. **Sample Size:** Minimum 1,000 visitors per variation
3. **Duration:** Minimum 1 week
4. **Primary Metric:** Conversion rate
5. **Secondary Metrics:** Bounce rate, time on page, scroll depth

### Test Sequence:
**Week 1-2:** Test format (question vs. benefit vs. social proof)
**Week 3-4:** Test winning format with different copy
**Week 5-6:** Test winning copy with different subheadlines
**Week 7-8:** Polish and optimize winner

### Success Criteria:
- **Minimum lift:** 5% conversion improvement
- **Statistical confidence:** 95% minimum
- **Consistent performance:** Over full week including weekends

### Elements to Test Beyond Headline:
- Subheadline copy
- CTA button text and color
- Hero image or video
- Social proof placement
- Trust indicators
- Form length/fields
