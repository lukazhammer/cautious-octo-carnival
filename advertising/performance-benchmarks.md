## Google Ads Benchmarks

### Search Campaigns

**Click-Through Rate (CTR):**
- Excellent: >8%
- Good: 5-8%
- Average: 3-5%
- Below Average: <3%

**Brandora Target:** 6%+ (brand strategy category typically performs well)

**Cost Per Click (CPC):**
- Brand Keywords: $2-$5
- Generic Keywords: $5-$12
- Competitor Keywords: $8-$15
- High-Intent Keywords: $6-$10

**Conversion Rate (Landing Page):**
- Excellent: >10%
- Good: 5-10%

- Below Average: <2%

**Brandora Target:** 8%+ for free assessment

**Quality Score:**

## Optimization Goals

### Month 1-3: Foundation Phase

**Primary Goals:**
- Achieve baseline performance across all channels
- CTR above industry average
- CPA under $300
- Conversion rate >5%

**Success Criteria:**
- At least 2 channels performing profitably
- Clear understanding of best-performing audiences
- Positive ROAS (>2:1) on at least one campaign

---

### Month 4-6: Optimization Phase

**Primary Goals:**
- CAC <$250
- LTV:CAC ratio >3:1
- Conversion rate >7%
- ROAS >3:1 on primary channels

**Success Criteria:**
- 3+ channels performing profitably
- Consistent month-over-month improvement
- Positive unit economics
- Predictable customer acquisition

---

### Month 7-12: Scale Phase

**Primary Goals:**
- CAC <$200
- LTV:CAC ratio >4:1
- Conversion rate >10%
- ROAS >4:1

**Success Criteria:**
- All major channels profitable
- Sustainable growth rate
- Strong channel diversification
- Optimized conversion funnel

---

### Year 2+: Mature Phase

**Primary Goals:**
- CAC <$150
- LTV:CAC ratio >5:1
- Conversion rate >12%
- ROAS >5:1

**Success Criteria:**
- Market-leading efficiency
- Brand awareness driving organic
- Lower paid dependency
- Consistent, predictable growth

## Red Flags & Warning Signs

**Immediate Action Required:**
- CAC >$400 (2X target)
- ROAS <1.5:1 (losing money)
- Conversion rate <3% (funnel broken)
- Quality Score <4 (Google Ads)
- CTR <0.1% (creative problem)

**Monitor Closely:**
- CAC $250-$400 (above target)
- ROAS 1.5-2.5:1 (marginal)
- Conversion rate 3-5% (below target)
- Increasing CPCs (auction competition)
- Declining CTRs (ad fatigue)

**Healthy Indicators:**
- CAC <$200
- ROAS >3:1
- Conversion rate >8%
- Stable/decreasing CPCs
- Stable/increasing CTRs
