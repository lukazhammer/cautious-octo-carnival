## Referral Program FAQ

**Q: How does the referral program work?**
A: Share your unique referral link. When someone signs up and makes their first purchase, you get $50 credit (or 1 month free Pro tier). They get 20% off their first purchase.

**Q: Is there a limit to how many people I can refer?**
A: No limit! Refer as many people as you'd like. The more you share, the more you earn.

**Q: When do I get my reward?**
A: Credits are applied within 24 hours of your referral's first purchase. You'll get an email notification when rewards are added to your account.

**Q: Can I cash out my referral credits?**
A: Referral credits can be used for any Brandora purchase or subscription. For 10+ successful referrals, contact us about cash payout options.

**Q: What counts as a "successful referral"?**
A: When someone uses your referral link to sign up AND completes their first paid purchase (Starter plan or above).

**Q: Can I refer my own company/team members?**
A: Team members don't count as referrals, but you can refer other companies or entrepreneurs. One referral credit per unique company.

**Q: What if my referral already has an account?**
A: Referral discount applies to new customers only. Existing customers can't use referral links, but they can become referrers themselves!

**Q: How do I track my referrals?**
A: Your referral dashboard shows all clicks, sign-ups, conversions, and rewards. Access it anytime from your account settings.
