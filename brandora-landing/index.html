Common Questions

How long does it really take?

About 30 minutes to complete the brand questionnaire. Your strategy is generated instantly after you submit. You can download all documents immediately and start implementing right away.

Is this AI-generated content?

Partially. We use AI to accelerate market research and competitor analysis, but the framework is based on our proven 7-step methodology from our published book. The AI applies human strategy, it doesn't replace it.

Can I edit or customize the output?

Absolutely. You receive downloadable PDF and editable documents. These are yours to modify, update, and use however you want. No restrictions.

What if I'm not satisfied?

30-day money-back guarantee on all paid tiers. If you're not satisfied with your strategy, email us for a full refund. No questions asked.

Do I need design skills to use this?

Not for Market Fit Pro (strategy only). For Design Ready, you'll get templates and guidelines, but you might want to hire a designer or use Canva to execute your visual identity. We provide the strategic direction and templates, not finished design work.

Can I upgrade later?

Yes. Start with Free or Market Fit Pro, then upgrade anytime. You'll only pay the difference between tiers.

Is there a subscription or recurring fee?

No. All tiers are one-time payments. Pay once, own forever. No monthly charges, no annual renewals. The only recurring cost is optional: if you want monthly template updates, we'll offer a subscription in the future.

What if my business changes or pivots?

You can purchase the Strategy Update Package ($47) to regenerate your brand strategy with new information. Or upgrade to a higher tier anytime.

Do you offer refunds if I don't use it?

Yes, within 30 days. Our guarantee is simple: if you're not satisfied for any reason, email us for a full refund.

Who is this right for (and not right for)?

Right for: Early-stage startups, solopreneurs, small teams who need strategic clarity fast and can't afford agencies.

Not right for: Enterprise rebrands requiring custom workshops, businesses needing ongoing consulting, companies with complex multi-brand portfolios.
